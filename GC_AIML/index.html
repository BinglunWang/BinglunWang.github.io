Goodenough College AI & ML

Welcome! We are the AI & ML Society at Goodenough College. We are a group of students and researchers who are passionate about artificial intelligence and machine learning. We host regular talks, port talk, and social events to promote the understanding and application of AI & ML technologies. We welcome everyone who is interested in AI & ML to join us.
